I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at UC San Diego, working with Arya Mazumdar. Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from UC Santa Barbara, where I was advised by Christos Thrampoulidis. I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics at Sharif University of Technology.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning Theory, Generalization and Optimization of Neural Networks
  • Transformers and in-context learning
  • Continual Learning
  • Distributed Optimization
  • High-dimensional Statistics
